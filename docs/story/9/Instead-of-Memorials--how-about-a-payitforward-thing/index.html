Instead of Memorials - how about a pay-it-forward thing??

By: Holly

Folks have been asking me if Mike had a particular organization for donations in his name. Nothing particular comes to my mind.

But how about if we did a "Pay-it-forward" sort of thing to honor him?

Find a person who needs your help, or a ride, or money, or cheering up, or needs dog-sitting  - whatever - and go a little out of your way to do something special for them. Then post on this site a story about what you did, or what happened. I think Mike would have liked to see a number of things on a very personal level blossoming out all over.
