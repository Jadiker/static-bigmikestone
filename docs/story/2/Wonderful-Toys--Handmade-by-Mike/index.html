Wonderful Toys - Handmade by Mike

By: Tracy

My Uncle Mike made some of my favorite toys when I was a kid.  He made me an ambulance about the size of a loaf of bread. He cut it out of wood, sanded it, painted it white and added all the decals and decorations. It had sturdy wheels. I played with it and drove it all around for years. He also built me a darker wood pickup truck. This was big enough for me to straddle and use my legs to drive around. He had my name spelled out in glittery letters on the front of the truck. He put real red reflectors on the back and white ones on the front. I rode that all around hauling my rocks, sticks and toys in the back.  As I grew so did my Uncle Mike toys. When I was 8 or 9 he made me a gorgeous cart that could fit two kids in the back and could be harnessed to my wonderful giant dog, Godel. Godel would pull my cousins and I around the block on this again and again. It was amazing! He was a man of many talents. I’m guessing most of you did not know what a talented carpenter/toy maker he was. -Tracy
