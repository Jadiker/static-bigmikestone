Missing Uncle Mike

By: London Lowmanstone

My Uncle Mike was one of the best adults in my life. He was caring, funny, and knew so much about so many different areas of life. It's rough to see him go.

My family and I wanted a way to collect people's stories about Uncle Mike alongside jokes you've heard or things you've learned from him. So, I built this site to help with that. I hope to learn from you all even more about who he was (and hopefully hear some crazy stories as well!)

Love,

London Lowmanstone (great-nephew of Mike Stone)
